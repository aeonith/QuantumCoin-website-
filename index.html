QUANTUMCOIN™

A quantum-resistant Proof-of-Work chain with a hard cap of 22,000,000 coins, a 2-year halving schedule spanning ~66 years, ~10-minute blocks, and RevStop™ — the owner-local safety circuit.

The Story

Quantum computing threatens to break today’s cryptography. QuantumCoin™ is designed from the ground up to secure digital money in the post-quantum world. It uses dilithium-based post-quantum signatures, keeping transactions safe from quantum attacks while preserving the decentralized Proof-of-Work model that Bitcoin pioneered.

Design Principles

  • Post-Quantum Security — Dilithium signatures resist quantum attacks.
  • Proof-of-Work Fairness — Mining keeps issuance decentralized.
  • No Premine — Transparent launch, fair distribution.
  • Predictable Policy — Bitcoin-like halving schedule, but tuned for 66 years.

Tokenomics

The monetary policy is simple and immutable:

  • Hard Cap: 22,000,000 QTC
  • Block Time: ~10 minutes
  • Halving: Every 2 years (for the first 50 years), then slows
  • Total Issuance Period: ~66 years

RevStop™ Safety

RevStop™ is a unique security layer allowing wallet owners to enforce local stop conditions. If your wallet is ever compromised, you can activate RevStop™ from your offline key or secure USB. This ensures coins remain under user control — not an attacker’s.

Nodes & Pruning

Full validation nodes maintain the network’s trustlessness. Pruned nodes allow smaller devices to participate without storing the full chain history — broadening decentralization while preserving security.
