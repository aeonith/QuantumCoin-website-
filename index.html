What is QuantumCoin?

QuantumCoin (QC) is a clean-room Layer-1 focused on durability. We keep Bitcoin-style simplicity where it matters (issuance, consensus) and adopt post-quantum cryptography where the future demands it.

  • Consensus: Proof-of-Work (permissionless, time-tested)
  • Supply: Hard cap 22,000,000 QC with predictable halvings
  • Security: Post-quantum signatures (future-ready)
  • Self-Protection: RevStop — opt-in, owner-local pause

Design Goals

  • Credible scarcity • auditable issuance
  • Simple node operation (archival, pruned, light)
  • Open standards • reproducible builds
  • Neutral monetary policy, no premine

Technology at a Glance

Post-Quantum Signatures

Modern cryptography designed to resist quantum adversaries while remaining practical for users and nodes.

Lean Node Profiles

Archival for explorers, pruned for everyday users (5–20 GB), and light/SPV for mobile.

Deterministic Economics

Bitcoin-like emission curve, fixed max supply, no inflation after the cap — easy to verify on-chain.

RevStop — Owner-Controlled Safety

RevStop lets a wallet owner temporarily pause their own outgoing transfers during emergencies (compromised keys, lost device). That’s it — no central seizure, no global kill-switch.

wQC — Wrapped QuantumCoin (IOU)

wQC is a fixed-cap ERC-20 (100,000 supply) redeemable 1:1 for native QC when mainnet launches. It enables early liquidity without inflating QC.

  1. Deploy wQC (100k total)
  2. List on a DEX (seed liquidity)
  3. Launch QC mainnet
  4. Redeem 1 wQC → 1 QC (public window)
  5. Sunset wQC, QC remains
See Roadmap (Contract link soon)

Why it works

  • Fixed supply prevents drift from QC scarcity
  • Unit-for-unit redemption (not USD-pegged)
  • Transparent burn → mint ledger at redemption

Node Profiles

  • Archival: explorers & researchers
  • Pruned: 5–20 GB typical (default)
  • Light/SPV: mobile and low-power

Mining

Anyone can mine QC by running a node and miner. Parameters target global participation and modest hardware requirements.

Testnet binaries and guides will be published prior to mainnet.

FAQ

Is wQC pegged to USD?

No — market sets the price. Redemption is 1:1 in units (wQC → QC), not dollars.

Is RevStop a central freeze?

No. It is opt-in and local to the wallet owner; no third party can seize funds.
