Introduction

What is QuantumCoin™?

QuantumCoin™ (QC) keeps Bitcoin-style issuance and decentralization, while upgrading signatures for a post-quantum world. Supply is hard-capped at 22,000,000 coins via Proof-of-Work—no premine, no discretionary inflation.

  • Post-quantum signature scheme
  • RevStop™ owner-local pause (opt-in emergency safety)
  • Node modes: archival, pruned, and light/SPV
  • Transparent economics, predictable halvings
