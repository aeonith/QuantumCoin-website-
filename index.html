Risk & Rationale

Why Post-Quantum Matters

Classical chains rely on elliptic curves (ECDSA). A sufficiently powerful quantum computer running Shor’s algorithm could derive private keys from public keys—enabling theft and signature forgery. Adversaries can also harvest now, decrypt later by recording today’s data and breaking it in the future.

Threat Landscape

  • Key Extraction: Public-key → private-key recovery destroys account safety.
  • Signature Forgery: Spend without the owner; breaks finality & trust.
  • Harvest-Later: Archive now, unlock later when quantum scales.
  • Timeline Unknown: Long-lived ledgers need defenses before quantum arrives.
QuantumCoin™ Approach

QC adopts post-quantum signatures (Dilithium class), keeps Proof-of-Work, and adds RevStop™—an owner-local, opt-in pause for key-compromise events. Pruned nodes reduce hardware cost so more people can validate.
