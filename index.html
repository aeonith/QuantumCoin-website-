Why Post-Quantum?

Big quantum machines could run Shor’s algorithm to extract private keys from public keys, forging signatures and draining wallets. Adversaries can also harvest now, decrypt later: record data today, break it tomorrow. QC moves to post-quantum signatures now, not after it’s too late.
